# QuickQuote — Pricing-call playbook

## Goal

Land on a number, in writing, that the prospect doesn't push back on inside the same call. Two outcomes are acceptable: a verbal "send the contract at $X" or a clear "we need approval from Y by Z."

## Pre-call

- Pull their LinkedIn headcount and last funding round
- Check whether they're on the free tier — if so, what they actively use
- Note their current vendor (if known)

## Opening (2 minutes)

Recap the problem they wrote in the form. Don't restate it — name what's underneath it. Most "we need a faster quote turnaround" is really "I'm losing deals to a competitor who responds in 4 hours."

## Anchoring (3 minutes)

Frame the pricing **before** they ask. Three tiers, top tier first.

> "For teams at your size we usually start the conversation around the Team plan, $25 per seat per month. The smaller plan is $15 — most teams outgrow it within the first quarter, but it's there if you want to start lean. Where would you want to land?"

## Closing

If they hesitate on number: ask what would have to be true for them to feel good about $25.

If they say "let me think about it": ask what specifically they want to think about. Vague hesitation = lost deal.
