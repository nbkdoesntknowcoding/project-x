01 / BRAND

Voice and mark.

Mnema is precise and quiet. The wordmark sits in 600 weight with tight tracking. The accent color appears at most twice per screen — a single signal of state, never decoration.

MNEMA / GLYPH

PRINCIPLES

01 / Restraint

One accent. One mono. Everything else earns its place by being structural.

02 / Density

Information rich, never crowded. Hairlines do the dividing work shadows would.

03 / Speed

Transitions cap at 160ms. Motion is for state changes, not delight.

02 / COLOR

Neutral surfaces. One accent.

A four-step neutral stack from canvas to surface-3, paired with three ink tones. The accent is the only chromatic token in the system — change its hue and the entire product re-skins.
