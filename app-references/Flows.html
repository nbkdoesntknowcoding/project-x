FLOWS

Compose context for your AI agents.

A flow is a sequence of content units — docs, instructions, snippets — that an AI agent reads in order. Each step has an instruction. Claude pulls them one at a time, reasoning through your context in the order you specified, not all at once.

New-hire onboarding Walks Claude through company tone, product overview, eng setup, and team contacts — in that order.
